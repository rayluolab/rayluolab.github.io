---
title: Binding free energies — MM/PBSA and alchemical methods
summary: GPU-accelerated MM/PBSA and robust free-energy perturbation for binding-affinity prediction.
date: 2026-06-03
---

We develop methods to predict binding free energies for drug discovery:
GPU-accelerated MM/PBSA for protein–ligand and membrane-protein systems, and
alchemical free-energy perturbation made robust by singularity-free softcore
potentials (DEGAUSS). The goal is accurate, efficient affinity prediction
across diverse targets.
