---
title: Allostery and disease-relevant protein dynamics
summary: Cryptic allosteric sites and functional mutations in signaling and cancer targets.
date: 2026-06-02
---

We use molecular dynamics and free-energy analysis to uncover cryptic allosteric
sites and to interpret disease-associated mutations in signaling proteins and
cancer targets (for example SHP2 and the Hippo-pathway kinases), connecting
conformational dynamics to function and therapeutic opportunity.
